the tiny zoo

the tiny zoo is a collection inspired by the pixel art style of tiny dinos

I wanted to challenge myself and see how many different animals I could represent in this pixel style while keeping them recognizable

While doing research I was shocked by some of the animals' populations, and realized that selling these tiny animals as NFTs was a perfect opportunity to raise money for wildlife conservation!

20% of all funds are donated to a variety of wildlife conservation efforts, including WWF, The Nature Conservancy, and zoo-led conservation programs.

Like many emerging NFT collections, all the art related to the tiny zoo (tiny animals, habitats, etc) are under a cc0 license, encouraging people to share and use the images however they like.

features of a tiny zoo animal

Each animal has 2 traits:

Background

Identifies in which habitat the animal can be found in the wild.

Status

The animal's official status (their threat of extinction) according to the IUCN Redlist

These traits are intended to help spread awareness about the current state of these animal populations. While many of us are aware of endangered species in a broad sense, I think many are unaware which species are threatened and how threatened they are.

tiny zoo map

the tiny zoo will have several releases: the initial opening, the habitat reveal, the marine exhibit, and a zoo expansion. In total the collection will be 100-200 items.

tiny zoo visitors (holders) will be able to influence future donations add animal additions by submitting their favorite charities and animals, as well as participating in polls.

donations

the tiny zoo has made three donations already! One donation went to the Toronto Zoo Conservation Program and the other two went to WWF (World Wildlife Foundation), towards the Koala and Sea Turtles.

rarities

As in the wild, there are fewer Critically Endangered animals in the collection as there are others, making the more endangered tiny animals rarer.
